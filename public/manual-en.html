1. The Study Dashboard

Track your learning progress, check card counts, view scheduled due cards, and analyze 7-day review forecasts at a glance. All database operations reside securely in your local browser storage.

2. Spaced Repetition Study Mode

Practice your seed idioms using the standard SuperMemo-2 (SM-2) algorithm. Click cards to trigger a elegant 3D flip revealing translations, cultural origins, and usage advice.

3. Interactive AI Sentence Practice

Compose an original sentence using your target idiom. Submit to Chrome Gemini Nano, Ollama, or high-fidelity offline models to receive a precise grammatical evaluation and score on natural flow.

4. Deck Manager & Local Backups

Quickly search, create, modify, or permanently delete vocabulary cards. Back up your learning history as secure Gzip Base64 mailto payloads, or handshake instantly to Yugawara cloud synchronization.
